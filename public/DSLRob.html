Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems Series

6 DSLRob workshops occured since 2010:

  • 2010 1st International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-10) in Taiwan, October 2010
  • 2011 2nd International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-11) in San Francisco (USA), September 2011
  • 2012 3rd International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-12) in Tsukuba (Japan), November 2012
  • 2013 4th International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-13) in Tokyo (Japan), November 2013
  • 2014 5th International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-14) in Bergamo (Italy), October 2014
  • 2015 6th International Workshop on Domain-Specific Languages and models for ROBotic systems (DSLRob-15) in Hamburg (Germany), September 2015

In 2016, a Special Issue on Domain-Specific Languages and Models for Robotic Systems was published in the JOSER journal: http://joser.unibg.it/index.php?journal=joser&page=issue&op=view&path%5B%5D=8
